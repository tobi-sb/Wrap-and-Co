Frequently Asked questions

Do you offer free car checks?

We are pleased to offer complimentary consultations to discuss your vehicle customization goals, whether it's a color change vinyl wrap, detailing service, or any other automotive enhancement. Our experienced team is ready to provide insights, answer your questions, and guide you through the possibilities.

How much time does a full service take?

For a more accurate estimate, we recommend reaching out to our team directly. During a consultation, we can discuss your specific requirements, assess the condition and size of your vehicle, and provide you with a detailed timeframe for completion. Our goal is to ensure each service is completed with the utmost precision and efficiency, delivering outstanding results within a reasonable timeframe.

What are your opening hours?

At Wraps and Co, we are open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service during these hours. If you have specific inquiries or would like to schedule an appointment, feel free to contact us during our operating hours, and we'll be happy to assist you.
